Single Hand Watches

First Watch

The first watch displays hours along the outer edge and minutes on the inner circle. To read the time, use the tip of the hand near the outer edge for hours and the opposite tip near the inner circle for minutes.

Second Watch

The second watch displays minutes along the outer edge and hours on the inner circle. To read the time, use the tip of the hand near the outer edge for minutes and the opposite tip near the inner circle for hours.
